About Me

I am probably considered to be nearing the end of my IT career, rather than just starting my career, as most of the other students are doing. But I like a challenge and new tech, so here I am, learning another language and some more web skills.

I consider myself to be very privileged to have worked through the era of huge and mind blowing developments in the computer industry - I started in 1982 as a computer operator at the South African Reserve Bank, after passing a logic test - I was actually going to go study commercial art in the new year.... The computer we worked on, and its "new" friend...not much smaller...were housed in a room probably the size of an average, modern one bedroomed apartment! These were mainframes, and they even had their own engineer, onsite, all the way from the US!!

I'm a tech at heart and not overly fond of just pure development work, but love being a maintenance and support person - whether on file servers, warehouse management systems, retail systems or even marketing systems. My main goal is to always keep production stable and secure. I love to learn new tech and love systems such as the voice directed picking system used in a warehouse....so I guess robotics and AI are probably my new best friends.
